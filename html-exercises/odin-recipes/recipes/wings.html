Buffalo Wings

Description

Chichen wings fried to perfection and tossed in your choice of wing sauce. Complimented by a side of fries and a cold beer.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Peanut Oil
  • Flour
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Buffalo Wing Sauce

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, and pepper. Coat the wings with the batter and fry until fully cooked.
  2. Pour the buffalo sauce into a bowl and add the cooked wings. Cover the top and shake until the sauce is evenly distributed.
