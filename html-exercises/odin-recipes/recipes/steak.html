Steak

Description

A Texan favorite - a thick and juicy steak cooked medium and served with your choice of sides.

Ingredients

  • Half inch cut of T-bone
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Generously season the steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Place the steak on the grill once it is already hot, 5 minutes each side.
  3. Brush butter and garlic on the steak throughout the grilling process.
